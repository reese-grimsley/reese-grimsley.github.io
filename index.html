Systems Engineering
Distributed Embedded Systems

Internet of Things, Cyber Physical Systems and Wireless Sensor-Actuator Networks are all examples of Distributed Embedded Systems with micro-controllers and sensors interacting with the physical world and each other. I see huge potential in these systems driving applications like widespread environmental monitoring and care, city-scale autonomous vehicle networks, and factories absent of human hands.

I have a detailed oriented mindset with an interest in systems engineering. My first two years doing a PhD have opened my eyes to a wide variety of topics and problems within this space. The opportunities within this degree allowed me to design, build, and deploy a real sensor network (EnviSense), create a programming models and distributed runtimes for soft real-time embedded applications (TickTalk Python), and prototype my own low-power sensors (CommonSense; including hardware/firmware design, PCB production, test and SMT, and teaching colleagues to use them in their own research projects). I could not ask for a better introduction to this field, but I'm itching to cut my teeth at a real systems engineering problem in industry!
